I LOVE FOOD.

Working out is big part of my life. I go to gym almost everyday.

I started my crazy diet life when I was 17. I thought of myself being "fat", thought I was not. I was 5'2 and about 106 lbs, size 0. However, I was so sure that I was too heavy and I went on crazy diet: 3 apples a day for a week in a row, or 21 days with only water and black coffee. I got so depressed by those crazy diet, and I felt I can never be happy again.

Fortunately, working out brought me out of the shadow of being on diet. The first time of running was, of course, horrible, but I got the hang of it after several weeks. Because of long distance running, I started to feel better about myself and stopped being on crazy unhealthy diet. I ate balaced meals and rest well, and of course, run, run, run.

Now I am 21, still 5'2 and 106 lbs. I am still running.

This is how I do things. I decided to do it, then I don't give up. I just do it. Right before I started to run the first time, I had never run more than 10 mins in my entire life. I told myself "Unless I am about to throw up or pass out, don't stop!". I am a tough cookie. I don't give up. I don't stop.
